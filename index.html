My favorite food places in NYC

Prosperity Dumpling ($)

The dumplings are juicy with a crispy skin. They taste great and are inexpensive.

Jack’s Wife Freda ($$$)

This hipster Mediterranean restaurant serves one of the best steak & fries I ever had.

Burger & Lobster ($$)

For $20 you get either lobster, lobster roll or a hamburger plus fries and a delicious side salad with every meal.

Cafe Habana ($$)

The plates of food are packed with flavor known for their grilled corn topped with chili powder, creme, cotija cheese and lime.

Max Brenner New York ($$)

Dessert heven. A wide variety of desserts inclduing a chocolate foutain for those with a sweet tooth

Bibble & Sip ($)

A little cafe with unique drinks as well as the best cream puff in the city!

10Below Ice Cream ($)

Creamy Thai rolled ice cream with your choice of any three toppings. One of the best ice cream shops in the city

Sweet Chick ($$)

The perfect place to have brunch. They serve the best chicken and waffles
